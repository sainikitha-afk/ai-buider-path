Internal Research Notes — Presidio Materials AI Project

Q1 Research Findings:
- Physics-informed models outperform traditional ML in small datasets
- Orowan-based features significantly improve prediction accuracy
- Rank-based alignment improves microstructure-composition mapping

Key Insights:
- Strength increases with decreasing inter-particle spacing
- Trade-off observed between strength and ductility
- Microstructure features are more predictive than composition alone

Project Direction:
- Focus on hybrid models combining physics and ML
- Improve interpretability of learned features
- Use regime-based validation instead of sample-level validation

Challenges:
- Data scarcity
- Noise in industrial datasets
- Lack of direct mapping between images and batches

Future Work:
- Incorporate deep learning for image feature extraction
- Expand dataset with synthetic data
- Improve robustness of predictions
